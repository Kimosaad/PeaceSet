# PeaceSet Privacy Policy

## Your Privacy Matters

PeaceSet is designed with privacy as a core principle. All your personal data stays on your device.

## Data We Collect

### Health Data (Optional)
- Sleep duration and timing
- Heart Rate Variability (HRV)

This data is read from Apple Health with your permission. It is used only to calculate your wellness score and is never uploaded to any server.

### Screen Time Data
- App usage patterns (via Apple's privacy-preserving APIs)
- Enforcement events (when limits are reached, shield interactions)

This data is used to enforce your restrictions and calculate your focus score.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your iPhone:
- In the app's private container
- In a shared App Group container (for extensions)

Data is encrypted by iOS and protected by your device passcode.

## Data We Don't Collect

- We do not upload personal data to any server
- We do not track your location
- We do not read your messages, emails, or app content
- We do not share data with third parties
- We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs

## Your Control

You can:
- Revoke permissions at any time in iOS Settings
- Delete all app data using "Reset all data" in Settings
- Uninstall the app to remove all stored data

## Wellness Disclaimer

PeaceSet is a wellness tool designed to help you build healthier digital habits. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical or mental health condition.

If you have concerns about your mental health, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.
